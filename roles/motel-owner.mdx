---
title: "Motel owner role"
description: "Motel owners register and manage their properties on Ubik. They have access to a dedicated dashboard for overseeing motels, rooms, and reservations."
---

The motel owner role (`roleId: 3847261094857362`) is assigned when a user registers through the owner registration flow. Owners can create motels, add rooms, and monitor bookings from their dashboard.

## Registration flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to account selection">
    Go to `/select-register` and choose the owner registration option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the owner registration form">
    The owner registration form is at `/register-owner`. This flow collects additional information compared to customer registration, including details needed for legal verification of the property.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in and access the dashboard">
    After registration, log in at `/login`. Owners are granted access to `/dashboard/owner`, which is protected by `permissionGuard('view_dashboard')`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What owners can do

- **Register motels** — Create a new motel listing at `/create-motel`.
- **Add rooms** — Add rooms to an existing motel at `/create-room`.
- **List properties** — View all registered properties at `/listProperty`.
- **View the owner dashboard** — Monitor motel count, today's reservations, and today's revenue at `/dashboard/owner`.

## Key routes

| Route | Guard | Description |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| `/dashboard/owner` | `permissionGuard('view_dashboard')` | Owner dashboard with motel and reservation summary |
| `/create-motel` | — | Register a new motel |
| `/create-room` | — | Add a room to an existing motel |
| `/listProperty` | — | List all registered motels |

<Warning>
The `/dashboard/owner` route requires the `view_dashboard` permission. If a customer or unauthenticated user navigates to this route, `permissionGuard` redirects them to `/home`.
</Warning>

## Owner dashboard

The owner dashboard at `/dashboard/owner` shows a real-time summary of the owner's portfolio:

- Total motels registered
- Reservations received today
- Revenue generated today

From the dashboard, owners can trigger actions to add a new motel or view the current reservation list.

## Permissions

Owners receive the following permissions in `PermissionService`:

```typescript
// src/app/core/services/permission.service.ts
3847261094857362: ['view_dashboard', 'create_room', 'view_reports'], // owner
```

| Permission | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| `view_dashboard` | Access the `/dashboard/owner` route |
| `create_room` | Create rooms within a motel |
| `view_reports` | View reporting data |

<Info>
Owners do not have `edit_users` or `delete_room` permissions. Those are reserved for admins.
</Info>

## Motel approval

When an owner registers a motel, it enters the approval queue with `approvalStatus: 'PENDING'`. The motel is not publicly visible until an admin sets the status to `APPROVED`. See the [Admin role](/roles/admin) page for details on the approval workflow.
